About the Server

The Industrial Age Is Yours to Shape

⚙

Gearforged World is a heavily modded Minecraft civilization server running on NeoForge 1.21.1. It blends the mechanical complexity of Create mod with the physics-driven engineering of Aeronautics — giving players the tools to build working factories, functional airships, and war machines unlike anything in vanilla Minecraft.

But technology is only the tool. The real game is civilization building: claiming territory, forging alliances through proximity-based diplomacy, declaring raids, and fighting for dominance during weekly PvP windows.

Join or found a civilisation. Build in the open — bunkers and underground bases are limited. Every structure you raise is a statement of power, and every enemy can see it.
